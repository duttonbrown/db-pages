Frequently asked

The short answers.

Who qualifies?
Interior designers, architects, design-build firms, set decorators, showrooms, and independent professionals actively working on design projects. If you spec fixtures for a living — or a big part of it — you qualify.
How fast is approval?
Most applications are auto-approved within minutes. Anything that needs a human look goes to Chase and gets a decision inside 48 hours.
Is the discount the same across all products?
Yes — 20% off retail on everything in the catalog. For larger, ongoing relationships, we build custom pricing programs. Chase will reach out if that's a fit.
Do you do project quotes?
Always. Email Chase with the fixture list, finishes, quantities, and project timing. Draft order back in 24 hours. Quotes are good for 30 days.
What about lead times?
Standard lead time is 6-10 weeks from order confirmation. Custom finishes or larger runs may add time — but we'll tell you before you commit, not after.
Can I get CAD or Revit files?
Yes. Every fixture has CAD, Revit, and PDF spec documentation. Downloadable from the product page once you're logged in as trade.
I've bought from you before on the old wholesale program. Do I need to reapply?
Nope. We've already migrated you. Check your inbox for a welcome email — your pricing is preserved and your account is ready to log in.
