About

Felix Esuna

Swift Blade Hair is more than a salon — it's a place where everyone belongs.

Felix Esuna has been crafting cuts and colors in Minneapolis for over a decade. His work is precise, personal, and always tuned to your vision. The space on Lake Street is intentionally eclectic: plants everywhere, a crystal ball on the shelf, a Frostmourne sword on the wall.

Swift Blade Hair has always been a welcoming, safe space for every identity in the neighborhood. Whether you're coming in for a transformation or just to feel good, you'll leave looking like yourself — only sharper.
