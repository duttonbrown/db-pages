Frequently asked

The fee, the terms, the fine print.

Why pay for Power Designer? Isn't trade supposed to be free?
Trade is free. Fifteen percent, spec sheets, samples, a named rep — all free. Power Designer is for specifiers who want priority, concierge service, and the physical kit. The $200 isn't a gate on the program — it's the cost of the kit, the priority production time, and the concierge SLA. Most Power Designers clear the math in their first order.
Can I start with Trade and upgrade later?
Yes. Upgrade any time — we'll prorate. Downgrade at renewal if it's not working. No lock-in.
What if I already bought through the old wholesale program?
You're grandfathered in. Your existing discount is preserved. You can stay on the Trade tier (free) or upgrade to Power Designer at any time. Check your inbox — we've already sent you a welcome-back note.
Do I pay the $200 before or after I'm approved?
After. Everyone applies to Trade free first. If you're approved and want Power, we charge the fee then. You never pay for an application that didn't go through.
What does "priority production" actually mean?
Our shop has a queue. Power Designer orders enter the queue at the front — typically 1-2 weeks earlier than standard. On a 6-10 week lead time, that's real. We'll tell you the revised ETA when you place the order, not after.
What's a "miss-spec return"?
Our fixtures are made to order, so we don't normally accept returns. Miss-spec returns are for the moments where a designer specifies the wrong finish, the wrong length, the wrong version — it happens. Power Designers get two per year at no restocking fee. We'll rework it into a new spec.
Is the founding-member rate for life?
The $149 rate applies to your first year. After that, standard $200/year. Founding members keep the badge forever — and the bragging rights.
How fast is approval?
Most approvals are automatic — minutes. Edge cases go to Chase for review, decided inside 48 hours. Power Designer upgrades are instant once Trade is approved.
Can my whole firm share one Power Designer membership?
Yes. One Power Designer fee covers the firm. Everyone on the team gets access under the same Shopify B2B Company. Add as many team members as you need.
What does the annual Power Summit cover?
A 60-minute virtual event each fall. What's launching next year, what we're seeing in the industry, Q&A with Thomas and the team. Replays go to all Power Designers who can't make it live.
