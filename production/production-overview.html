View:
Day = Wed May 6. Week = Apr 30 – May 6. Month = Apr 7 – May 6.
The right number in each cell is the queue change (received minus fulfilled). Sockets is qty × socket count, derived from Lighting SKUs. Pillows excluded.

Three views — toggle above the matrix:

  • Past (default): trailing/completed windows. Day = yesterday. Week = last 7 days ending yesterday. Month = last 30 days ending yesterday. Numbers don’t change while you watch them. Best for the morning standup and accountability.
  • Current: since-midnight windows. Day = today (midnight to now). Week = this Mon to today. Month = the 1st of this month to today. Numbers grow through the period and reset at each boundary. Best for “where are we right now in this period?”
  • Rolling: now-anchored windows. Day = today so far (the cache stores per-calendar-day, so Rolling Day == Current Day; we cannot split sub-day). Week = last 7 days ending today. Month = last 30 days ending today. Best for comparing throughput across snapshots taken on different days of the week.

Received counts each line item by the order’s created_at date. Cancelled orders are skipped entirely. Drafts are never counted.

Fulfilled counts items by the date Shopify recorded a fulfillment event. Partial shipments count their fulfilled qty on the day each event happened.

Past Day uses the last Dutton Brown business day (weekends and observed company holidays skipped per the Team Handbook). Current and Rolling Day both mean today.

Excluded: pillows, bulbs, package protection, expedite fees, custom-color line items, signature requirement, rework fees, return labels.
